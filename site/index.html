Summer School :: Key Insights on Networks and Graphs :: June 22 - 26 2015

Key Insights on Networks and Graphs

A summer school on spectral graph theory, distributed computing, social networks, with algorithms and applications.

Leukerbad - Switzerland, June 22 - 26, 2015

Important dates

Registration : 11 March - 1 May 2015

Summer school : 22 - 26 June 2015

Program

The details of the program will be available soon, but we can already provide some information.

  • The summer school will start on Monday 22nd of June 2015, at noon.
  • Each speaker will give a 4-5 hours lecture in multiple time slots.
  • Half a day will be devoted to an excursion in the mountains.
  • The summer school will end on Friday 26th of June 2015, in the evening.
